The Heaven & Earth Grocery Store by James McBride is a literary mystery book. This story begins when a skeleton is found in a well in a predominantly Black and Jewish neighborhood in Pennsylvania. The book travels between the years, showing the story of the person behind the skeleton, as well as the current day people finding out the story of it. This book focuses on the themes of racism, community, and justice.

Published on August 8, 2023.
Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Literary Fiction, Mystery
